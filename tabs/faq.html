FAQs

Please CONTACT us if you have additional questions
Can my vehicle be tuned back to stock?

Yes, vehicles can be restored to factory configuration.

Will this voide my warranty?

While the platform and calibrations are developed for off road and competition use, we have never been informed of any warranty concerns. Should you every have a concern after tuning we provide free reversions to stock for all our customers. To date this has never been needed for any engine/transmission related warranty concern. Like any aftermarket or modification, if it is proven to be the cause of a fault then your warranty could be voided. But, just because a tune is on the vehicle does not mean it is voided.

Does my Engine/Trans ECU software need to be updated (Toyota Service Bulletin TSB)?

We will verify your truck's software version and update at no additional costs if required.

Can my Speedometer be adjusted?

We do not offer speedometer calibration. There are a few speedometer calibrator on the market. Check out Rough country and Hypertech.

Is the tune detectable by the dealership?

The tunes do not modify the OEM ID that is displayed when scanned. This means it reports back as the "stock" calibration would using the standard Toyota calibration tools.
